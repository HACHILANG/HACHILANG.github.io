Object Orientation and Scripting

We all know the pain of trying to find a good, fast Object Oriented programming language while also making it so simple a child could use it. Well Pain No More with HACHI.

Simple and Fast

HACHI is one of the most simple languages out there, a child with little coding experiance could use it. It is very fast and you will enjoy it's speed, if you want exact stat's put "stats" at the after you specify your file name to run.

It has a lot of features, from now on stop using 1000 different modules for 1 command. We most likely have it pre-added. Not there. With what we give you it should be simple to do it, or find a module. It is very powerful as well, even though it is simple(as said before), it is very powerful, and that gets helpful real quick.
